Everything you need to know about being a great Barista

1. What is a Barista?

The word Barista comes from the Italian language. A barista is a person who makes coffee and serves customers. They are espresso machine coffee artists who have extensive knowledge about coffee. They prepare, decorate and serve drinks to customers.

A barista works in a coffee shop and is responsible for cleaning and sanitizing work areas, seating areas, equipment and tools. They also take customer orders and process payments.

2. Main Duties of a Barista

  • Preparing and serving hot and cold drinks such as coffee, tea, artisan and specialty beverages.
  • Cleaning and sanitizing work areas and equipment.
  • Cleaning serving and sitting areas.
  • Describing menu items and suggesting products to customers.
  • Ordering, receiving and distributing stock supplies.
  • Receiving and processing customer payments.

3. Barista Knowledge

A barista must have a deep understanding of coffee blends, the art of coffee roasting, operating and maintaining espresso machines, and the delicate skill of frothing and steaming milk.

Important Barista Skills

  • Customer Service – interacting with customers, taking orders and receiving payments.
  • Knowledge of coffee.
  • Attention to detail.
  • Team work.
  • Multi-tasking.
  • Working with a team.

How to Be a Better Barista

  • Consistency
  • Proper knowledge
  • Grooming
  • Maintenance and managing
  • Cleanliness
  • Informative interactions

Customer Service – Main Key for Barista

  • Attentiveness
  • Knowledge
  • Flexibility
  • Grooming
  • Push sales ability
